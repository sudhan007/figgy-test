Figgy Delivery Privacy Policy

Figgy Delivery is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you use our app, how we use that information, and how we protect it.

Information we collect

We collect the following information from you when you use our app:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your delivery address
  • The restaurants you order from and the food you order
  • Your Firebase token for notification purpose only

How we use your information

We use your information to provide you with our food delivery service. This includes processing your orders, delivering your food, and providing you with customer support. We may also use your Firebase token to send you notifications about your orders and other relevant information.

How we protect your information

We use a variety of security measures to protect your information, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. We also limit access to your information to authorized employees and contractors.

Sharing your information

We do not share your information with any third-party services.

Your choices

You have the following choices about your information:

  • You can opt out of receiving notifications by unsubscribing in the app settings.
  • You can request to access or delete your information by contacting us at [email protected]
